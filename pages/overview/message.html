Founder's Message

I started Trivanta Foundation in 2021 with a simple thought — if we see a problem, we should try to solve it. The first time I felt this deeply was when I saw a street animal injured in an accident. That incident pushed me to act, and later we began giving collars to street animals to protect them. From there, the idea of doing something bigger for people and communities grew inside me.


Today, through Trivanta Foundation, we are working on computer education, healthcare for tribal people, waste management, environmental care, and awareness building. My dream is to see communities become self-reliant, healthy, and united, where even the smallest effort can bring a big change.


— Anis Ibne Hoque Pinku Founder, Trivanta Foundation
