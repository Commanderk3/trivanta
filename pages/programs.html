Our Programs

Computer Education Center

Through our Computer Education Centers, we provide skill-based training in digital literacy and computer applications to youth and underprivileged communities. The project aims to enhance employability, bridge the digital divide, and empower individuals with the tools needed to succeed in today’s technology-driven world.

Medical Dispensary for ST Communities

Trivanta Foundation runs a medical dispensary dedicated to tribal (ST) communities, ensuring accessible and affordable healthcare for marginalized groups. Our focus is on providing basic medical facilities, awareness on health practices, and support for vulnerable populations who often lack access to proper treatment.

Waste Management & Environmental Care

We are committed to protecting the environment through waste management initiatives, plantation drives, and awareness programs. This project promotes sustainable practices, community participation, and environmental responsibility, aiming to create a cleaner and greener future for all.
